Visit Nigeria

Nigeria is a West African country with a population of over 200 million beautiful and vibrant people with diverse cultures and languages.

Nigeria is bounded to the south by the Atlantic Ocean and to the north by the Sahara Desert. There are 36 States in Nigeria with Abuja as the capital city. Other major cities with exciting activities include Lagos, Port Harcourt, Kano and Imo. Nigeria is a multicultural country with over 250 ethnic groups and about 500 different languages, which are largely grouped into three major ethnic groups/launguages namely Igbo, Hausa, and Yoruba. The weather in Nigeria is moderate with temperature ranges 19 - 39 degrees celsius. This presents a very suitable environment for several fun activities for tourists.

2. Yankari Game Reserve

If you are an animal and nature lover, this is just the place for you! Yankari National Park is a large wildlife park located in Bauchi State. It is home to several natural warm water springs, as well as a wide variety of flora and fauna. Its location in the heartland of the West African savanna makes it a unique way for tourists and holidaymakers to watch wildlife in its natural habitat.

Idanre Hills

Idanre Hills is located in an ancient city set upon a hill in the city of Idanre Akure, Ondo State. Ensure you take the 682 steps it takes to get to the top of the hill and bask in the beautiful landscape of the city with your family.
